Pokémon Detective Pikachu By Rob Letterman

DESCRIPTION

The story begins when ace detective Harry Goodman goes mysteriously missing, prompting his 21-year-old son Tim to find out what happened. Aiding in the investigation is Harry's former Pokémon partner, Detective Pikachu (2019): a hilariously wise-cracking, adorable super-sleuth who is a puzzlement even to himself.
